The Best Movies According to Kendall

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avengers Endgame

This movie had some of the best action sequences and it wrapped up a 10 year journey with so many characters in a really amazing and emotional way.

The Avengers

The first Avengers movie will always be on my top 3 list of best movies as it has some of the best lines and interactions with the starting heros of the MCU.

Deadpool & Wolverine

This movie made me laugh so much, and I have watched it almost four times since the movie released in theaters.
